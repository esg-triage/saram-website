Privacy

Privacy at a glance.

We keep this simple, and we keep it private.

No cookies, no trackers

This website sets no cookies and uses no analytics, advertising or tracking scripts. Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts CDN; no other data about your visit is shared with third parties.

What we collect

We only receive the information you choose to send us. When you use the contact form, your name, company name, email address, the practice you selected, and your message are transmitted to our mail server at saramdigitech.com, which delivers your enquiry to info@saramdigitech.com. We do not store form submissions beyond what is needed to send the email. If you email or call us directly, we use your details solely to respond to your enquiry.

How we use it

We use your information only to reply to you and to discuss a possible engagement. We do not sell it, share it with third parties, or use it for marketing without your agreement. We retain it no longer than needed for that purpose.

Your choices

To ask what we hold about you, or to have it deleted, email info@saramdigitech.com. We will respond promptly.
