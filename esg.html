Division 02 — ESG Consulting

An ESG program that is credible, measurable and real.

From materiality to verified disclosure.

ESG is no longer optional. With BRSR and CCTS in India and CBAM, CSRD and buyer audits (PSCI, EcoVadis) in Europe, verified data is now a condition of market access. We help you build a program that holds up to scrutiny and drives real operational change — energy audits, GHG accounting, product carbon footprints and BRSR readiness — backed by empanelled energy auditors and accredited GHG verifiers.

Per-product carbon data for CBAM, buyers and tenders.
