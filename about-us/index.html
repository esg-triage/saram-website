Our Story

“At Saram Digitech, we began with a vision to revolutionize procurement and supply chain management. Founded by passionate professionals, we aim to fill the industry’s void of personalized understanding.

Our journey is rooted in the belief that technology should be a guiding force, not just a tool. We set out to redefine SAP Ariba and SAP S4HANA/ECC solutions by offering more than just services – we provide empathy and guidance. Our mission is to help businesses innovate and grow.”

Our Values

Our company name “Saaram / Sāram” is a word derived from Telugu, which translates to ‘essence’ or ‘core’. We strive to be the essence of success for your procurement and supply chain digital transformation. Here are the core values that form the crux of our business.

Quality First

We are committed to delivering nothing less than excellence. Quality is the cornerstone of our services, and we consistently strive to exceed expectations. Our dedication to quality ensures that every project we undertake is executed with precision and finesse.

Empathy and Understanding

We recognize that every business is unique, with its own set of challenges and aspirations. Our approach is grounded in empathy and a deep understanding of your pain points. We walk in your shoes to provide tailored solutions that address your specific needs.

Continuous Support

Our commitment doesn’t end with project completion. We offer comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) to provide ongoing support, ensuring your systems run smoothly and efficiently.

Trust

We believe in fostering strong partnerships with our clients. We don’t just offer services; we collaborate with you as a trusted partner on  your digital transformation journey. Your success is our success.
