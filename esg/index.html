Practice 02 · Corporate ESG

An ESG programme that is credible, measurable and real.

From materiality to verified disclosure.

ESG is no longer optional. With BRSR and the Carbon Credit Trading Scheme in India, and CBAM, CSRD and buyer audits (PSCI, EcoVadis) in Europe, verified data is now a condition of market access, and the requirements are reaching down to suppliers. For sustainability and finance leaders, that is both a reporting burden and a commercial risk. We help you build a programme that holds up to scrutiny and protects your revenue: energy audits, GHG accounting, product carbon footprints and BRSR readiness. We prepare you; independent third parties verify.
