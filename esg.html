Services

What we deliver

Our ESG practice is grounded in science-based methodologies and designed for the practical realities of industrial operations.

01 — GHG Accounting

Rigorous greenhouse gas inventory development aligned to GHG Protocol Corporate Standard and ISO 14064-1.

S1
Scope 1 InventoryStationary combustion, process emissions, fugitive releases, and mobile sources across all facilities.
S2
Scope 2 InventoryMarket-based and location-based electricity accounting, with renewable energy certificate (REC) tracking.
S3
Scope 3 Value ChainMaterial upstream categories (purchased goods, capital goods, fuel use) and downstream categories relevant to your sector.
MR
Monitoring & Reporting SystemData collection templates, activity-data registers, emission-factor databases, and calculation audit trails.

02 — Product Carbon Footprint (PCF)

Product-level carbon accounting aligned to ISO 14067 and the GHG Protocol Product Standard — the foundation for CBAM declarations and customer disclosure.

•
System Boundary & LCA DesignDefine the life cycle stages, allocation rules, and data sources for a defensible PCF study.
•
CBAM Embedded EmissionsCalculate direct and indirect embedded emissions per tonne for CBAM-covered products: steel, cement, aluminium, chemicals, fertilisers.
•
Customer-Facing PCF ReportsStructured PCF disclosures for OEM, export, and tender requirements, in formats aligned to customer and buyer expectations.
•
Decarbonisation LeversHotspot analysis identifying the highest-impact reduction opportunities across the product life cycle.

03 — Supplier ESG Programs

Build a credible supply-chain sustainability program that satisfies customer audits, procurement policies, and emerging regulations.

•
Supplier Sustainability AssessmentQuestionnaire design, scoring methodology, and risk tiering for your supplier base.
•
Scope 3 Supplier Data CollectionEngagement templates and guidance to collect primary activity data from key suppliers.
•
Supplier DevelopmentCapacity-building workshops and improvement roadmaps for strategic suppliers with high ESG risk.
•
Policy & Code of ConductSupplier code of conduct, ESG contract clauses, and procurement policy alignment.

04 — Audit-Ready Disclosure

Sustainability reporting that is structured, evidenced, and built to withstand third-party verification.

•
BRSR Core & ExtendedIndia’s Business Responsibility and Sustainability Reporting framework — mandatory disclosures and extended indicators.
•
GRI Standards ReportingMaterial topic selection, disclosure mapping, and GRI-aligned sustainability report drafting.
•
TCFD / Climate Risk DisclosurePhysical and transition risk assessment, scenario analysis, and TCFD-aligned disclosures.
•
Third-Party Verification ReadinessEvidence file preparation, data trail documentation, and pre-assurance gap assessments.
