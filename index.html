What we deliver

Sustainability compliance is now a condition of doing business.

In India, BRSR and the Carbon Credit Trading Scheme; in Europe, CBAM, CSRD and buyer ESG audits — reporting and verified carbon data have moved from optional to mandatory. Our ESG practice gets you audit-ready: energy audits, GHG accounting, product carbon footprints and BRSR readiness, plus the in-house green-skills capability to sustain it.

01
Scope 1–3 GHG AccountingA full greenhouse-gas inventory on the GHG Protocol, including supply-chain (Scope 3) emissions.
02
Product Carbon Footprints (ISO 14067)Per-product footprints for CBAM, buyer and net-zero tender requirements.
03
Supplier & Value-Chain ESG DataCollect supplier ESG and emissions data from your existing ERP or e-procurement tools.
04
Audit-Ready DisclosureBRSR, CCTS and CSRD-aligned data prepared for independent verification.
